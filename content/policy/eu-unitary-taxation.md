*With Miroslav Palanský.*

**Presented at IIPF 2024.**

Using country-by-country reporting data, we estimate the revenue consequences of EU-wide unitary taxation. The reform would raise US$24.1–26.8 billion in isolation, and more when combined with a minimum corporate tax, benefiting most EU member states.
