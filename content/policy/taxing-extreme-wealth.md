*With Miroslav Palanský.*

**Presented at the Paris School of Economics Conference.**

We analyse the wealth-tax revenue potential of 173 countries, calibrated on Spain's model, and estimate a potential global revenue of approximately US$1.9 trillion from progressive net wealth taxation.
